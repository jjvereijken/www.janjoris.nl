Cats

These are our two cats Piet and Juul. Piet is a black puss, Juul is a grey-white striped tom. They where named after Princess Juliana, former Queen of The Netherlands, and her son-in-law Pieter, since all four mammals share April 30 as their birthday. Yes, we know we got the genders wrong.

Below you'll find a few pictures taken in July of 1999, when they were about 9 weeks old.

By the way, the really big cat on the last picture is my wife Tieleke.

UPDATE:

Juul died on February 4, 2002, in a traffic accident.
Piet died on March 22, 2007, of natural causes.
